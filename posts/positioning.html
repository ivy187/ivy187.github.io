IVY 187 的定位 The Positioning of IVY 187

IVY 187 是一个用系统思维理解世界的内容组合。我们持续讨论四件事：电力、科技、投资、商业。看似分散的话题，在我们这里会被放到同一张地图里理解，因为它们在真实世界中本来就是相互连接的。 IVY 187 is a content collective that tries to understand the world through systems thinking. We keep returning to four themes: power, technology, investing, and business. These topics may appear separate, but we place them on the same map because they are deeply connected in the real world.

为什么是这四个主题 Why these four themes

电力决定工业文明的底层运行方式，科技塑造工具与生产力边界，投资决定资源如何配置，商业则决定技术最终能否形成自洽的现实闭环。把这四件事分开看，容易得到局部结论；放在一起看，才更接近真实世界。 Power determines how industrial civilization operates at a foundational level. Technology expands the boundary of tools and productivity. Investing determines where resources flow. Business determines whether a technology can close the loop in the real world. Looking at these domains separately often produces local conclusions; seeing them together gets us closer to reality.

知识星球延续什么精神 What spirit the Knowledge Planet extends

星球延续的是 Ivy 187 一直以来的气质：纯粹、硬核、求知、长期主义。你会在这里看到中美两地的一线行业观察，AI 工具与自动化工作流的真实实践，基于工程思维的投资分析，以及技术如何走向产业与商业闭环的深度拆解。 The Knowledge Planet community extends the spirit that has always defined Ivy 187: rigor, curiosity, intellectual honesty, and long-term thinking. Here you will find first-hand industry observations from China and the United States, practical use of AI tools and automation workflows, investment analysis rooted in engineering thinking, and deep breakdowns of how technology reaches industrial and commercial closure.

我们希望建立的关系 The relationship we want to build

我们不把内容做成单向灌输，也不把讨论简化成短平快结论。IVY 187 更希望和读者建立一种长期共学关系：一起跟踪行业变化，一起训练判断框架，也一起把复杂问题说清楚。 We do not want content to be one-way broadcasting, nor do we want discussion reduced to short and shallow conclusions. IVY 187 aims for a long-term learning relationship with readers: tracking industry change together, refining judgment frameworks together, and making complex problems understandable together.
