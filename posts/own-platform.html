IVY 187 的起点 The Beginning of IVY 187

故事始于田纳西诺克斯维尔的 Ivy 187。几位电气工程 PhD 曾在这里无数次深夜长谈，从电力系统到能源转型，从科研方法到产业落地，也从技术一路延伸到投资、商业与 AI 工具。 The story began at Ivy 187 in Knoxville, Tennessee. Several electrical engineering PhDs spent countless late nights here talking through power systems, energy transition, research methods, industrial execution, and eventually investing, business, and AI tools.

那些讨论并不只是知识交换，更像一种共同的方法训练。我们习惯把问题拆开，把系统看全，把局部现象放回更长的时间线里理解。久而久之，Ivy 187 变成了一个精神坐标。 Those conversations were more than information exchange. They became a shared training ground for method: break problems apart, see the whole system, and place local events back into a longer timeline. Over time, Ivy 187 became a shared point of orientation.

如今，我们站在不同位置 Now we stand in different places

现在，我们分处中美两地，所处位置也更复杂了。有人在工业现场看技术怎样真正落地，有人在产业与资本之间判断技术如何形成商业路径，有人从投资和 AI 工具切入真实决策过程，也有人持续追踪新型电力系统的行业演进。 Today we work across China and the United States, and our vantage points have diversified. Some of us watch technology land in real industrial settings, some study how technologies become viable business paths between industry and capital, some approach decisions through investing and AI tools, and some continue to track the evolution of next-generation power systems.

视角虽然不同，但问题意识是一致的。我们都对“技术如何走向产业与商业闭环”这件事有持续兴趣，也都相信系统思维比碎片化判断更可靠。 Our perspectives differ, but the underlying questions are shared. We all care about how technology reaches industrial and commercial closure, and we all believe systems thinking is more reliable than fragmented judgment.

IVY 187 的精神没有变 The spirit of IVY 187 did not change

纯粹、硬核、求知、长期主义，这四个词直到现在仍然适用。它们既是内容风格，也是判断标准。我们不追求表面的热闹，而更在意能否形成更稳定的认知框架与更经得起推敲的分析。 Rigor, curiosity, intellectual honesty, and long-term thinking still describe us well. These are not only stylistic preferences, but standards for judgment. We do not optimize for surface-level noise; we care more about building durable mental models and analyses that can withstand scrutiny.

为什么要做这个站点 Why build this website

公众号和知识星球分别承担公开表达与深度讨论，但我们仍然需要一个属于自己的公开首页，把我们是谁、讨论什么、从哪里出发，清楚地放在一起。这个站点不是为了替代其他平台，而是为了给所有内容一个稳定坐标。 Our WeChat account and Knowledge Planet community serve different roles: public expression and deeper discussion. But we still needed a front page of our own, a place that clearly states who we are, what we discuss, and where we come from. This site is not meant to replace other platforms; it gives all of our work a stable point of reference.

IVY 187 不是临时性的内容试验。它更像一个长期项目，持续记录我们对电力、科技、投资与商业的理解，也欢迎与你在更长的周期里相遇。 IVY 187 is not a temporary content experiment. It is a long-term project that keeps recording how we think about power, technology, investing, and business, and we hope to meet readers over a much longer horizon.
